# Physics Sim 0.2.0-alpha.2

Release date: 2026-07-11

This complete-water-sandbox prerelease expands the validated recovery core into a coherent creative sandbox, challenge set, curated gallery, and engineering laboratory.

## Highlights

- Water pouring now uses conservative active-stream regularization with protected physically supported droplets.
- Connected volume rendering adds deterministic foam, spray, impact accents, depth shading, and restrained highlights without changing solver state.
- Advanced Tools restores directional and omni emitters, gates, sensors, drains, pumps, and valves with selection, editing, undo/redo, replay, and scene-v2 persistence.
- Three deterministic challenges provide visible criteria, hold progress, budgets, restart, completion, and failure feedback.
- The versioned gallery offers eight reviewed Learn, Sandbox, and Challenge scenes with current-renderer thumbnails and category navigation.
- Seven responsive procedural audio layers cover pouring, flow, impact, devices, and objectives with mute/volume controls and device-loss recovery.

## Validation

Balanced and quality solver profiles retain mass, momentum, pressure, collision, density, long-run, and determinism gates. Fast, Standard, and Full tiers cover unit, integration, solver, visual, replay, challenge, gallery, audio, smoke, benchmark, and packaging evidence. The release ZIP is generated from protected `main`, carries SHA-256 checksums and licenses, and is launched from a clean directory.

## Compatibility and limitations

- Pre-recovery and alpha.1 scene/save/settings/replay compatibility is not guaranteed; current repository content uses scene v2, replay v2, and recovered-settings v1.
- Windows x64 is the only packaged platform.
- The CPU solver targets deterministic, plausible 2D water rather than 3D, GPU, multiphase, or research-grade behavior.
- The gallery is deliberately curated; legacy placeholder and numerically weak scenes are excluded.
- Challenge completion is not a persistent campaign or account progression system.
